Privacy Policy

How Gurudatta Trading Co. collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and Spring Rate Calculator.

Last Updated: 30 June 2026

📋 Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Gurudatta Trading Co. ("we", "us", "our"), based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India, collects, uses, and safeguards the information you share with us when you visit our website or use our online Spring Rate Calculator. By using this website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

📥 Information We Collect

When you use our Spring Rate Calculator to request a quote, we collect the following details that you voluntarily provide:

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • Spring design inputs you enter (wire diameter, outer diameter, free length, coils, material type, etc.) — used only to generate your calculation results and quote

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as payment details, government ID numbers, or financial information through this calculator.

🗄️ How We Store Your Information

The information you submit through the Calculator (Name, Mobile Number, Email, and spring specifications) is stored securely in a private Google Sheet maintained by Gurudatta Trading Co. This data is accessible only to our internal team and is used solely to follow up with you regarding your spring enquiry and quote.

🎯 How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected only for the following purposes:

  • To prepare and respond to your custom spring quote request
  • To contact you via phone, WhatsApp, or email regarding your enquiry
  • To maintain internal records of customer enquiries
  • To improve our products and services

🚫 No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, lease, or share your personal information (Name, Mobile Number, Email) with any third-party advertisers, marketing companies, or external organizations. Your data is used exclusively for Gurudatta Trading Co.'s own business purposes — responding to your enquiry and providing you a quote.

We may disclose your information only if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request from government or regulatory authorities.

🔐 Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and while we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

🍪 Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to improve browsing experience (such as remembering your selected unit of measurement or form preferences). These cookies do not collect personally identifiable information and do not give us access to your device beyond what you voluntarily share.

⚖️ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data from our records
  • Withdraw consent for us to contact you, at any time

To exercise any of these rights, simply contact us using the details below.

🔗 Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to external websites (such as WhatsApp). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
